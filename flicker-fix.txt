If RTX or any NVIDIA cards on Arch giving flickers just simply turn off adaptive sync in settings if Plasma KDE Settings or any third party apps

if hyprland or any window manager on monitor.conf or conf file use vrr,0

For instance:
monitor=desc:GIGA-BYTE TECHNOLOGY CO. LTD. G27FC A 0x00000B57,1920x1080@165,0x0,1.2,vrr,0
